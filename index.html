About Me

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics and Statistics at MIT.

I fancy topics related to Development Economics, Empirical IO/Market Design, and Public/Labor Economics. Additionally, I am interested in Econometrics.

Previously, I was an Instructor at Universidad de Chile. I also worked at The National Bureau of Economic Research (NBER) and Microsoft Research as a Predoctoral Fellow.
